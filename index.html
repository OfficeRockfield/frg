ENVIRONMENTAL & INDIVIDUAL USE FACTORS
TEMPERATURE DO NOT USE ABOVE 35°C (95°F)
MOBILITY+ MAY DELIVER FASTER FLOW RATES AT HIGHER TEMPERATURES
DO NOT USE BELOW 15°C (59°F)
MOBILITY+ MAY DELIVER SLOWER FLOW RATES AT LOWER TEMPERATURES
HOW FEEDING POUCH IS POSITIONED DO NOT USE 15CM HIGHER THAN THE STOMA
MOBILITY+ MAY DELIVER FASTER FLOW RATES WHEN POSITIONED HIGHER THAN THE STOMA
DO NOT USE 15CM LOWER THAN THE STOMA
MOBILITY+ MAY DELIVER SLOWER FLOW RATES WHEN POSITIONED LOWER THAN THE STOMA.
TEMPERATURE DO NOT FILL MOBILITY+ TO LESS THAN 250 ML (8.5 OZ)
FILLING MOBILITY+ TO 250ML (8.5 OZ) MAY GIVE FASTER FLOW RATES THAN FILLING TO 500 ML (16.9 OZ).
DO NOT FILL MOBILITY+ MORE THAN 500 ML (16.9 OZ)
WEIGHT ON FEEDING POUCH PUTTING A HEAVY WEIGHT ON THE FEEDING POUCH, OR SQUEEZING THE FEEDING POUCH IS NOT RECOMMENDED AS IT MAY CAUSE FASTER FLOW RATES
